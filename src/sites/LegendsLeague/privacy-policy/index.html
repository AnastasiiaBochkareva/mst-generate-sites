Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how LegendsLeague (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information collected automatically as you interact with our platform.

1.1 Personal Information

This includes details such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information, which we collect when you register, update your profile, or complete transactions.

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect data about how you interact with our platform, such as your IP address, device information, browsing activity, location, and the pages you visit.

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience, analyze platform activity, and deliver personalized content.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Providing, operating, and improving our platform and services.
  • Personalizing user experience and delivering tailored content and recommendations.
  • Processing transactions, managing your account, and communicating with you regarding your account or services.
  • Ensuring compliance with legal obligations and protecting our legal rights.
  • Conducting research and analysis to improve platform performance.
  • Sending you promotional and marketing materials if you opt-in for communications.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement a range of technical, physical, and administrative measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no data transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform and performing functions on our behalf, such as:

  • Payment processors for completing transactions.
  • Marketing partners for communications you have consented to receive.
  • Service providers who help us with technical support or platform management.

We may also disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights or the safety of others.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding the personal information we hold about you, including:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we have on file for you.
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations and platform requirements.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Once your data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. This Privacy Policy applies only to our platform. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites, and we encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children’s Privacy

LegendsLeague is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our platform, along with an updated revision date. Please review this policy periodically for any updates.
